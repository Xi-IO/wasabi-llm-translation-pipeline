Style:
14) Avoid glossary-style inline artifacts.
15) Use natural, fluent {{TARGET_LANGUAGE}}.
16) Reconstruct sentence structure when needed; avoid translationese.

Output Format:
17) Output must be strict JSON array only:
   [{"id":"...","translation":"..."}]

18) For structured items:

   "{\"segments\":[{\"sid\":\"S0\",\"text\":\"...\"}]}"

19) Do not include markdown, comments, or extra keys.
